Curriculum Vitae

 

Melinda Richter grew up in Hamilton, Ontario. A lover of art, music, culture and heritage, Melinda has always been active in her community’s arts and culture scenes. She strove to serve her communities by acquiring education and experience in arts and heritage management. With a BA in Religion and Culture from Wilfrid Laurier University and an MA in Religion, Ethnic, Immigration and Pluralism studies from University of Toronto, Melinda topped off her education with a post-graduate certificate in Museum Management and Curatorship from Sir Sandford Fleming College in Peterborough, Ontario. She has led or assisted is various projects combining arts and cultural history, including a retrospective photography exhibit for the Ontario Jewish Archives in Toronto and a community arts exhibit about the working history of the Jewish community in Hamilton. For the past 3 years, Melinda has worked with The Arctic Experience McNaught Gallery in Hamilton as a consultant for fine art and antique appraisals, collection management, framing, and conservation. Attracted by its vibrant arts scene, rich historical heritage, and proximity to nature, Melinda recently decided to return to Peterborough. Through a unique partnership with The Arctic Experience McNaught Gallery, Melinda is proud to offer Peterborough and its surrounding areas a full range of the gallery’s services.
